Key Insights
Zero Wins
Per the workflow diagram (Win = $0 Attrit final), no attrition event in Q1 FY27 Commerce Cloud was fully eliminated. Every actionable record resulted in a Loss — either a complete logo churn, a partial save, or a Signature/Premier downgrade.
Competitive Threat: Shopify
Shopify is the named competitor in 3 of the 17 records: Cole Haan ($1.7M — logo loss), Lands' End ($300K — bridge), PartyCity.com ($472K — lost). Combined: $2.4M in confirmed Shopify-driven attrition.
Data Quality Crisis
88% of records (15/17) are non-compliant with PACE hashtag guidance on the renewal side. On the Red Account side, 0 of 12 ECOMM Headlines contain a valid offense hashtag — no offensive plays are formally tracked. 6 accounts have completely empty CSG Notes. 3 accounts that ended in full logo loss had NO Red Account and NO CSG Notes — these were visibility blind spots.
Story Threshold
Only 7 of 17 records meet the diagram's story process threshold (>$500K Premier / >$250K Signature). These 7 represent $12.1M (91%) of total attrition and should be prioritized for formal Win/Loss stories.
Area Distribution
RCG dominates with 13 of 17 records (76%) and $10.8M (80%) of total attrition. CMRCL accounts for 4 records ($2.6M, 19%). MAE has zero Commerce Cloud attrition events this quarter. All 3 logo losses are RCG accounts; the single largest non-actionable event (Otto Environmental, $1.8M) sits in CMRCL.
