New: Insight Boxes

Signature Customers: 8 Records | $5.51M Attrition — Highest Revenue-per-Record

Signature accounts represent 24% of records but 31% of total attrition ($5.51M). Key losses: SharkNinja ($1.07M implementation failure), JM Family ($916K), PetSmart ($533K WON RFP — partial save). These are our most-invested customers — losses here signal a failure of the Signature value proposition.

Data Integrity Flag: 7 Accounts Show Premier_Attached=True but Support Level=Basic

Landry's, Arbonne, Fanatics, BI-LO, Bethlehem, MBI Inc., and Hot Topic have IsPremier_Attached__c = true on the renewal but SupportLevel__c = Basic on the Account. Possible causes: plan lapsed before renewal closed, data lag, or field sync issue. Recommend validating with CSMs.

Basic-Tier Accounts: 11 Records | $5.44M — No Success Investment, Predictable Churn

Basic accounts have no Premier/Signature plan attached. These losses are harder to prevent without proactive engagement. Consider: Should high-ACV Basic accounts (e.g., Hot Topic $1.65M combined) be flagged for Premier attach before renewal?
