Methodology Definitions & Divergence
Category Definitions (per Win/Loss Workflow Diagram)
CategoryCriteriaDescription
Not ActionableSwing = $0Attrition was confirmed and non-savable — no CSM lever existed. Includes off-cycle restructures, financial distress, org transfers, and AE-confirmed non-savable events. Exits the workflow before Win/Loss classification.
WinsActionable + $0 Attrit FINALRisk was identified (Forecasted Attrition > $0) and the CSM fully eliminated the attrition — final outcome is $0 lost. The customer renewed flat or grew.
LossesActionable + >$0 Attrit FINALRisk was identified and the outcome was a loss. Sub-classified into:
• Logo Loss — Prior ACV fully attrited; customer is gone.
• Partial Save — Some ACV retained, some attrited; customer stays at lower spend.
• SP Downgrade — Signature/Premier tier removed but license ACV retained.
#CompliancePACE Attrition Hashtag GuidanceValidation against the PACE Attrition Hashtag Guidance document. Each record is checked across two objects:
• Red Account:
  — ECOMM Headline: Must contain valid offense hashtag(s) for active/completed plays (#SIGTRIAL, #ARI, #SWAP, #IMPLEMENT, #ADOPT, #RFP, #REVIVE). If play is rejected/declined, hashtag must be removed and noted in Latest Update.
  — Latest Update: Must contain history of plays — submitted, active, rejected — and explain what was attempted.
  — Closure Summary: Must be populated at time of closure (CSM + Escalation Manager). Confirms what worked and documents what didn't.
  — Competitor: Primary & Other Competitor fields must be populated if a known competitor exists.
  — APM (L1, L2, L3): Must identify which cloud/product is at risk. For Agentforce/Data Cloud, APM L1 = AI & Data.
• Renewal Opportunity:
  — CSG Notes: Must follow format: (Date) (CSM Name) (License hashtag) (Signature hashtag if Premier) (comment).
  — Valid defense hashtags: #LicNoAttrit, #LicPartialAttrit, #LicFullAttrit, #SigRisk, #NoSigRisk, #RA, #AFAttrit, #AfNoRisk, #D360Attrit, #D360NoRisk, #PBI.
