Methodology

Data Source

SOQL query against Org62 Opportunity object. Filters: Targeted_Clouds__c INCLUDES ('MC ExactTarget', 'Marketing Cloud', 'Pardot', 'Datorama'), CloseDate May 1 – Jul 31 2026, Forecasted_Attrition__c ≤ -100000, StageName IN ('05 Closed', 'Dead Attrition'), Account.CSG_Region__c = 'AMER PACE'. Total records returned: 14 (9 from 05 Closed, 5 from Dead Attrition).

Stage Composition

This report includes two stages: 05 Closed (9 records — standard closed renewals) and Dead Attrition (5 records — confirmed losses where renewal did not occur). Dead Attrition records are classified as Logo Loss unless CSM notes indicate otherwise (e.g., Winston Brands = Not Actionable due to bankruptcy).

Classification Framework

StepQuestionLogic
1Risk?Forecasted Attrition > $0 → Yes (all qualify)
2Actionable?Out of business / bankruptcy / non-primary cloud / off-cycle forecasting = Not Actionable
3OutcomeWin (≥90% rate + CSM confirms retention) | Logo Loss (0% rate or confirmed full exit / Dead Attrition) | Partial Save (partial retention) | SP Downgrade (license retained, Sig/Premier removed)

Cross-Cloud Records

5 records include multiple clouds (84.51: Core/Pardot/Industry; Rich Products: Core/MC; Fanatics multi: MC/Commerce; CSI Compressco: Core/Pardot; Abacode: Pardot/Own). Attrition is the total opportunity amount, not split by cloud. These may also appear in Core or Commerce reports.
