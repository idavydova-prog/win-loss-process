Methodology

Data Source

SOQL query against Org62 Opportunity object. Filters: Targeted_Clouds__c INCLUDES ('Commerce Cloud', 'B2B Commerce', 'Order Management'), CloseDate Feb 1 – Apr 30 2026, Forecasted_Attrition__c ≤ -100000, StageName IN ('05 Closed', 'Dead Attrition'), Account.CSG_Region__c = 'AMER PACE'. Total records returned: 18.

Stage Filter Note

This report includes BOTH '05 Closed' and 'Dead Attrition' stages to capture all finalized attrition events. Dead Attrition (9 records) represents confirmed non-renewals where the customer did not renew at all. 05 Closed (9 records) represents renewals that closed with some attrition component. Together these provide the complete picture of Q1 Commerce Cloud attrition outcomes.

Classification Framework

StepQuestionLogic
1Risk?Forecasted Attrition > $0 → Yes (all qualify)
2Actionable?Off-Cycle Forecasting, Non-Coterminous, bad debt, or bankruptcy forecasting = Not Actionable
3OutcomeWin (CSM confirms full retention / pre-signed renewal / ≥90% rate) | Logo Loss (0% rate, Dead Attrition, confirmed full exit) | Partial Save (partial retention with some ACV lost)

Dead Attrition Records (9 of 18)

Otto Environmental, Cole Haan, Nebraska Furniture Mart, Claire’s (Off-Cycle), Claire’s (B2B/OMS), Gap Inc, Riboli Family Wines, Dunham’s Athleisure, Land O’Lakes, and Hallmark Cards are all Dead Attrition stage. These represent customers who did not renew at all — no contract was signed. They are classified as Logo Loss unless they fall into the Not Actionable category (off-cycle/bad debt).

Cross-Cloud Records

8 records include multiple clouds (Core + Commerce variants). Attrition is the total opportunity amount, not split by cloud. These may also appear in the Core report.
