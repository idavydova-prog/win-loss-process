Methodology

Data Source

SOQL query against Org62 Opportunity object. Filters: Targeted_Clouds__c INCLUDES ('Commerce Cloud', 'B2B Commerce', 'Order Management'), CloseDate May 1 – Jul 31 2026, Forecasted_Attrition__c ≤ -100000, StageName IN ('05 Closed', 'Dead Attrition'), Account.CSG_Region__c = 'AMER PACE'. Total records returned: 9 (3 from 05 Closed + 6 from Dead Attrition).

Two-Stage Methodology Note

This report includes BOTH “05 Closed” (finalized renewals) and “Dead Attrition” (confirmed losses where the opportunity was closed as a complete loss). Dead Attrition records represent cases where the customer provided notice of non-renewal and the opportunity was removed from the pipeline. All 6 Dead Attrition records have License_Renewal_Status = “Will Attrit” and Forecasted_Renewal_Rate = 0% (full platform exits).
